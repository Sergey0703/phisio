Reflexology

Reflexology is a holistic therapy that supports wellbeing on physical, emotional, and psychological levels. By applying gentle pressure to specific points on the feet, hands, or ears—areas traditionally believed to correspond with different parts of the body - reflexology encourages relaxation, helps release tension, and promotes a sense of overall balance. Many clients find that reflexology creates a calming space where stress can ease, emotional wellbeing can be supported, and the body’s natural restorative processes can function more effectively. Through deep relaxation and improved balance within the body, reflexology can help strengthen the mind and body’s ability to support their own healing and resilience. Reflexology is also often chosen by clients undergoing cancer care as a comfort-focused complementary therapy. With the consent of their GP or consultant, reflexology may help individuals manage the stress, tension, and general discomfort that can accompany cancer treatments, offering a gentle and nurturing source of support during a challenging time. As a soothing mind-body practice, reflexology offers a pathway to greater harmony, grounding, and inner strength.

About Me

I have always been passionate about helping people enhance their life experiences through complementary therapies. I qualified as a General and Paediatric Nurse in 1991 and have worked across a wide range of patient care and management settings, including medical, surgical, paediatric, geriatric, and dementia care services. I have a strong interest in continuing education and evidence-informed approaches, ensuring that the therapies I offer are grounded in the best available knowledge to support my clients’ wellbeing. My formal journey into complementary therapies began in 2003 when I qualified in Massage Techniques. Since then, I have expanded my expertise to include Reflexology, Aromatherapy, energy-based therapies such as Reiki, Integrated Energy Therapy (IET), Emotional Freedom Techniques (EFT), Mindfulness and Meditation practices, Laughter Yoga, and most recently Hypnotherapy combined with CBT techniques. Over time, I developed my practice into a fully integrative approach, recognising that each client has unique needs and goals. My work is collaborative, with clients guided to actively participate in identifying and choosing the therapeutic approaches that are most appropriate for their personal journey. This ensures that every session is tailored to achieve the best possible outcomes for physical, emotional, and psychological wellbeing.

We provide a calm, nurturing environment where you can relax and let the healing begin. Each session is tailored to your individual needs, ensuring the most beneficial experience possible.
